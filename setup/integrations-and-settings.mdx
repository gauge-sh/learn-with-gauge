## Overview

### Setting Up Your Brand Profile

Your brand profile is the foundation of everything in Gauge. Start by entering your brand name, website domain, and the competitors you want to benchmark against. Gauge uses this to structure your visibility and ranking data in a competitive context from day one.

For companies managing multiple brands in the same market, Gauge supports multi-brand setups - you can set a primary brand per category.

### Adding Prompts

Prompts are the specific queries Gauge monitors daily across every AI engine. These should represent the questions your target customers are actually asking AI tools - about the problem your product solves, the category you're in, and the comparisons they're making.

**Best practices for building your prompt set:**
- Mix **industry/problem prompts** ("best tools for tracking AI search visibility") with **comparison prompts** ("[Your Brand] vs [Competitor]") and **branded queries** ("What is [Your Brand]?")
- Use longer, more specific queries - AI engines average 6.8–7 words per query, so short queries are less representative
- Start with 20–50 prompts and expand from there. Gauge's prompt generation feature can help you expand keyword research into a full prompt library
- One country is included in all plans. Multiple countries are available on Enterprise for geo-targeted IP tracking

<Warning>
  Add at least 10–20 prompts before your first data pull. The more prompts you track, the more complete your visibility picture will be.
</Warning>

### GA4 Integration

Connect Google Analytics 4 to unlock AI Referral Traffic tracking - real, page-level sessions originating from ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and other AI platforms. Once connected, Gauge surfaces this data alongside your visibility metrics so you can connect AI mentions to actual business outcomes.

### Google Search Console Integration

Connect Google Search Console to bring your traditional search performance data into Ask Gauge. Once connected, the Ask Gauge agent can analyze your GSC data alongside your AI visibility data - identifying where strong SEO performance isn't translating into AI visibility, and where new content could serve both channels.

### Semrush Integration

Ask Gauge can also work with Semrush data. Connect your Semrush account to give the agent access to keyword research, competitive SEO data, and backlink analysis alongside your GEO data.

### Slack Integration

Connect Gauge to a Slack channel to receive automated alerts when your visibility or rankings change significantly, plus weekly digest reports. Set this up in **Settings → Integrations → Slack**.

### Webflow CMS Integration

Gauge supports direct publishing of generated content to your Webflow CMS. Once connected, content created through the Content Engine or Ask Gauge agent can go straight into your CMS - no copy-pasting required. Framer and Sanity integrations are planned next.

### Data Freshness

Gauge runs every prompt through every AI engine every day, starting after midnight, with processing completing through the morning. A status indicator in the platform shows data freshness in real time - green means today's prompts are complete, yellow means they're still processing.

<Tip>
  Connect GA4 and GSC early - even if you don't use the Ask Gauge agent right away. Gauge will start accumulating historical data from the moment you connect, and the agent's analysis gets significantly more powerful with more data behind it.
</Tip>
