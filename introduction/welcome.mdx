## Welcome to Gauge

The world of search is changing. Every day, more and more people turn to AI for answers, and the brands that show up in those answers win.

Gauge is here to help you be the answer for AI.

## What Gauge Does

Gauge tracks hundreds to thousands of prompts across every major large language model. From there, it helps you understand exactly where you're mentioned, where you're cited, and how you stack up against your competitors.

Once you understand your position, Gauge finds your gaps, gives you your next course of action, and helps you execute on content.

**Track. Understand. Act.** That's Gauge.

## What You'll Learn in This Course

This course covers the fundamentals of AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) and shows you how to make the most of the platform.

### Section 1: Introduction

Understand how AI models search, what metrics matter, and how to think about improving your visibility in AI-generated answers.

### Section 2: Setup

Connect your integrations and configure your workspace so Gauge has everything it needs to work for you.

### Section 3: Navigating Gauge

A full walkthrough of every feature in the platform, from Rankings and Prompts to Ask Gauge and ChatGPT Ads.
