## Overview

### What is Gauge?

Gauge is a Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) analytics and action platform that helps brands improve their visibility in AI-generated search responses. The platform monitors hundreds of customized prompts daily for each client across all major AI engines - ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, Gemini, and Google AI Overviews - then aggregates and analyzes the resulting answers and citations to deliver specific, data-driven recommendations on what to do next.

The core differentiator is a closed-loop system: **data → action → measure**. Gauge finds the highest-value action based on rich underlying data, helps you execute it, and then measures the results. Where most tools offer visibility dashboards and leave interpretation to you, Gauge tells you what to do, why it will work, helps you do it, and shows you whether it worked.

The positioning is simple: **playbook, not dashboard.**

### Who is Gauge for?

Gauge is purpose-built for growth marketing teams, SEO professionals, and content marketers who need to win in AI search - not just track it. If you're in one of these situations, you're in the right place:

- **"We're invisible in AI search despite strong SEO rankings"** - Gauge shows you exactly why and what to do
- **"Our competitors are showing up in AI answers and we're not"** - Gauge tracks them daily and surfaces the gaps
- **"We know we have a GEO problem but don't know what to do about it"** - the Action Center and Ask Gauge agent do the analysis for you
- **"We want one place to analyze AI search data alongside our GA4 and GSC data"** - Ask Gauge unifies everything
