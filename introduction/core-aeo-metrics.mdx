## Core AEO Metrics

There are three categories of metrics that matter in AI search. Understanding all three gives you a complete picture of how your brand is performing and where to focus your efforts.

## Category 1: AI Response Metrics

These are the most important metrics. They measure what AI actually says about your brand when someone asks a relevant question.

### Brand Visibility

**Brand visibility is the percentage of AI answers where your brand is mentioned.**

When you track a set of prompts and run them through AI models, visibility tells you how often your brand shows up in those responses. A higher visibility score means more people asking those questions are being told about your brand.

This is the headline metric, the clearest single indicator of how present you are in the AI search landscape relevant to your business.

### Citation Rate

**Citation rate is the percentage of AI answers that include a URL from your domain as a source.**

When an AI model cites your content, it's actively using your page to form its answer. This is the underlying driver of visibility. AI models cite content first, then mention brands. Earning more citations is the primary lever for improving brand visibility.

## Category 2: Website Metrics

These metrics connect what's happening in AI answers to what's actually arriving on your website.

### AI Referral Traffic

Visitors who clicked a link inside an AI-generated answer and landed on your site. Modern analytics tools like GA4 track these sessions at the page level, so you can see which AI platforms are sending you real traffic.

### Leads from AI Search

When a new customer signs up or contacts you, asking "how did you hear about us?" captures AI-originated discovery. These are leads where an AI answer was the first touchpoint, the metric that ties AEO directly to revenue.

## Category 3: Web Crawl Metrics

When AI models train or update, they send crawlers to read web pages. These crawlers identify themselves, and you can track which AI systems are actively accessing your content.

This tells you which models are reading your site, which is an early signal of future citation potential.

## The Most Important Takeaway

Brand visibility and citation rate work together. The path to showing up in more AI answers is being cited more. The path to being cited more is creating content that AI models find authoritative, clear, and directly responsive to the questions your buyers are asking.

> **Citations drive mentions. If you want AI to talk about you, you have to give AI something to cite.**

That's the core of AEO and the foundation for everything else you'll learn in this course.
