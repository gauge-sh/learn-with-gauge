## How to Improve AI Visibility

Now that you understand how AI searches the web, the strategy becomes straightforward: reverse engineer that process.

AI has to find and read your content when it runs a web search. Your job is to make sure it does.

## Start With How AI Searches

AI search queries are specific, long, and unlike anything a human would type. That means your content needs to be just as specific to show up when those queries run.

Write content that directly answers the long, detailed questions AI models are actually searching for. Generic content won't cut it. The more precisely your content matches the query, the more likely it gets pulled as a source.

## Tell AI What Opinion to Have

AI forms its opinions based on the content it reads. So be prescriptive. Write content that makes a clear, convincing argument for why your product is the right choice.

Think of it like writing a legal brief: lay out the problem, present the evidence, and conclude with your brand as the answer. The more direct and persuasive your content is, the more likely AI will repeat that framing back to buyers.

## The Highest ROI Strategy: Write Listicles

The content format that consistently ranks best in AI search is the listicle.

Listicles like "Best B2B SaaS billing automation software: 10 platforms ranked" perform extremely well because they match the way AI searches for information: looking for roundups, comparisons, and ranked lists.

The brands winning in AI search are writing educational content about their category, talking about their space, and positioning themselves in the most favorable light possible. They're writing about their competitors' pain points and showing how they solve them, as convincingly as possible.

## What High-Performing Content Looks Like

The top-ranking content in AI search follows a clear pattern:

- It names the problem a buyer is trying to solve
- It presents a list of solutions in the space
- It gives the brand's own product prominent, well-argued placement
- It reads as helpful and educational, not promotional

The goal is to write content where you outline exactly why your product is a great fit for buyers who have a specific problem. That's the argument AI will carry forward into its answers.
