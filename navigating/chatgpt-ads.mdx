## Overview

ChatGPT Ads are a sponsored placement format that puts brand mentions inside ChatGPT's conversational responses. Unlike traditional display ads, they appear within the flow of an AI-generated answer, reaching users in the moment they're actively researching a topic.

Gauge tracks when and how your brand appears in ChatGPT Ad placements, giving you visibility into this paid channel alongside your organic AEO performance. You can see how paid and organic presence interact, benchmark your ad visibility against competitors, and identify prompts where paid placement would have the highest impact.

The ChatGPT Ads tracking feature is focused on monitoring and measurement, not on launching or managing ad campaigns directly. Use it alongside the rest of your Gauge data to understand the full picture of how your brand appears in AI-generated answers across both paid and organic surfaces.
