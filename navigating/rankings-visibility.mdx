## Rankings & Visibility

The Rankings page is the highest-level view of your AI presence. It's usually the first place to check when you log in. It gives you an immediate read on how your AEO is trending and how you compare to competitors.

## What's on This Page

### Your Brand Coverage

At the top of the page, you'll see your overall brand coverage, the percentage of AI answers across your tracked prompts where your brand appears. This is your top-line visibility number.

### The Brand Section

On the right side of the page is a list of all the brands showing up across the prompts you're tracking. For each brand, you can see their visibility and the sentiment AI models assign to them when mentioning them.

You can mark any brand as a direct competitor using the bullseye toggle. Once selected, competitors appear in a dedicated competitor view for clean side-by-side comparison.

### Filtering by Topic and LLM

At the top of the page, you can filter by topic group or by specific AI model. These filters let you take unique views of the data, seeing how your visibility looks in a specific category, or how you perform on ChatGPT versus Gemini versus Perplexity.

### Prompt Examples

At the bottom of the page, you can browse individual prompt cards to see exactly how AI models responded to a specific question. Clicking into a prompt shows you:

- The exact question that was tracked
- The full AI-generated response
- Where your brand was mentioned and what sentiment was given
- The citations the model used to generate that answer

### Top Domains and Citations

The Rankings page also surfaces the top domains being cited across your tracked prompts, as well as the most frequently cited individual URLs. This shows you which sources AI models treat as authoritative in your space.
