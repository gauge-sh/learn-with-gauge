## Ask Gauge

Ask Gauge is your marketing agent for organic, paid, and AI search. It connects all of your data, Google Analytics, Search Console, ads, keyword research, and AI visibility, and helps you act on it without leaving the platform.

## What Ask Gauge Does

### Reporting and Analysis

Ask Gauge can pull performance reports across any time period, surfacing how your visibility, traffic, and rankings are trending. Rather than digging through individual dashboards, you can ask a direct question and get a synthesized answer grounded in your actual data.

### Gap Identification

The agent analyzes your data to surface gaps you might not see on your own: topics where you're not showing up, keywords worth targeting, or areas where competitors are outpacing you. These recommendations are based on real keyword research and AI visibility data from your connected integrations.

### Content Creation

Once a gap is identified, Ask Gauge can take you from analysis all the way to content. It can create a content brief, generate an outline, and write a full article, all informed by your search data and company context.

## How It Works

Ask Gauge understands all of your Gauge data alongside anything you've shared about your company. The more context you give it through your integrations and settings, the more accurate and on-brand its output will be.

You can ask it anything related to your search presence and it will use your actual data to respond, not generic advice.
