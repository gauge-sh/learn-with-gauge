## Overview

### What is Ask Gauge?

Ask Gauge is Gauge's AI marketing co-pilot. It's the primary interface for the full workflow from tracking to analysis to content creation. Most competitors surface data in dashboards and leave you to figure out what it means. Ask Gauge does the analysis for you: it identifies what matters, explains why, and recommends what to do about it.

The agent has access to everything in your Gauge account:
- AI visibility data (brand mentions, citation rates, rankings, prompt-level answers)
- Google Analytics 4 (AI referral traffic, session data at the page level)
- Google Search Console (organic search performance)
- Semrush data (keyword research, competitive SEO data)
- Company context through Brand Books (your positioning, product, and market fit)
- Ad data

This makes Ask Gauge a unified marketing intelligence layer where you can analyze all your search data together, not just your AEO data in isolation.

### Ask Gauge vs. the Action Center

The Action Center surfaces prioritized recommendations based on your data. Ask Gauge is better at telling you what to do next — because it can analyze across all your data sources simultaneously, ask follow-up questions, and adapt its recommendations based on your company context.

For most teams, running the full AEO workflow through Ask Gauge is faster and more effective than navigating between individual views.

### What to Ask

Ask Gauge handles any analytical or content-creation task related to your search data. Some high-value starting points:

**Analysis and strategy:**
- "Why is my competitor increasing in visibility this month?"
- "Which prompts have the lowest visibility and what should I do about them?"
- "Where are my biggest citation gaps right now?"
- "How has my visibility changed over the last 30 days and why?"

**Content creation:**
- "Write a content brief for a listicle targeting [topic]"
- "Create a content calendar for the next 4 weeks based on my current gaps"
- "Draft an article about [topic] optimized for AI visibility"

**Prompt management:**
- "Analyze my current prompts and suggest 10 new ones I'm missing"
- "Which tracking gaps do I have in [category]?"

**Cross-channel analysis:**
- "Where am I ranking well in Google Search Console but not appearing in AI answers?"
- "Which pages are getting AI referral traffic in GA4?"

### Company Context and Brand Books

Ask Gauge uses stored company context — your positioning, writing style, product information, and market fit — as part of every analysis and content generation task. This is managed through Brand Books. The more context you give the agent, the more accurate and on-brand its output will be.

Set up your Brand Book before using Ask Gauge for content creation. It makes a significant difference in the quality of the output.

<Tip>
  Use Ask Gauge to prep for team meetings. Ask "summarize my top wins and biggest AEO gaps from the last week" and share the output directly with your team. It takes 30 seconds and gives you a data-backed status update without digging through dashboards.
</Tip>
