## Overview

### Managing Your Prompts

Your prompts are the core of your Gauge tracking setup. Each prompt represents a query your target customers are likely asking AI tools. Gauge runs every prompt through every configured AI engine every day, stores the responses, and tracks how your brand and competitors appear over time.

From the Prompts view you can:
- Add new prompts individually or generate them from keyword data
- Organize prompts into Topics (thematic groupings) for aggregated analysis
- Filter prompts by model, date range, or visibility status
- See per-prompt visibility scores and ranking data

### Reading the Actual AI Responses

The prompt detail view shows the verbatim AI-generated answer for each tracked prompt across each engine. This is one of the most valuable views in Gauge. You can see exactly what ChatGPT, Perplexity, or Gemini says when someone asks a question relevant to your brand or category.

Use this view to:
- Audit whether your brand is being described accurately
- See exactly how competitors are being positioned relative to you
- Identify the specific language and framing AI engines use for your category
- Spot answers where your brand is absent but should appear

### Citation Rate vs. Mention Rate

This distinction matters a lot.

**Citation rate** is how often an AI engine pulls one of your URLs as a source when generating an answer. It's a strong signal that AI engines treat your content as authoritative.

**Mention rate** is how often your brand name actually appears in the generated answer text when your content is cited. AI models frequently use your content to inform their answer but don't credit you by name. Your content shapes the response, but the reader never sees your brand.

A high citation rate with a low mention rate is a common pattern. The fix is to refresh your cited content with brand-linked examples, proprietary data, and first-person perspectives that make it harder for models to reference your content without naming you.

### Query Fan Out Visibility

This is a unique Gauge feature that shows you the actual Google searches ChatGPT runs in the background when processing one of your tracked prompts. It reveals how AI engines "think" — what queries they run, what content they're discovering, and how they synthesize results into an answer.

Use Query Fan Out to understand what's shaping the AI response for a given prompt, and what content you'd need to rank for in order to influence the answer.

### Citation Gaps

A citation gap is a prompt where a competitor is being cited but your brand is not. These are your highest-priority content opportunities. Gauge surfaces them clearly in the Citations view so you can identify exactly where to focus your content creation efforts.

<Tip>
  Pages that earn consistent citations are your most powerful AEO assets. Keep them updated, well-structured, and directly responsive to the question the prompt is asking. First-party blog content is the most reliable format for influencing how AI engines perceive your brand.
</Tip>
